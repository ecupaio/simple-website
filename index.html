Just a simple website

As a web developer with over a decade experience, I've heard the phrase "It's just a simple website" quite a bit so I decided to make a simple website to demonstrate what that actually looks like.

This website is the epitome of simplicity. It has no framework or CMS. This website is simply, HTML and a little bit of CSS.

You won't be able to edit this website nor will you be able to add new pages, however this website is mobile responsive, scores a perfect 100 on Lighthouse, and meets all WCAG accessibility standards.

From the domain purchase, design, copywriting, coding, analytics setup, and launch, it took me 2 hours to complete. At my contractor rate of $100 per hour, it cost $200 to create this site.

Although this is both time and cost effective, it probably isn't what you had in mind for a simple website. The fact is websites are highly complex products that serve many different users: both visitors and editors, as well as enable you or your organization to achieve it's goals.
